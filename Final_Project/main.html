Where the Idea Came From

Whispering Dead was inspired by the ambitious yet disappointing game The Day Before. Initially, The Day Before captivated survival game enthusiasts with promises of a vast, immersive world filled with intense mechanics and dynamic encounters. However, after entering alpha, players quickly realized that the game was far from completion, leading to mounting concerns. As delays persisted and the developers failed to provide substantial updates, many began to suspect that the project was a scam. Eventually, the developers deleted the game and absconded with players’ money, prompting Steam to issue refunds to hundreds of thousands of frustrated customers. Recognizing the gap left by this unmet potential, Im making a game called "Whispering Dead" to deliver the gripping and realistic survival experience that players had hoped for and wanted. By focusing on stealth-based gameplay and a fully dynamic environment, Whispering Dead aims to fulfill the promises that The Day Before could not, ensuring players a rewarding and immersive journey.

All Games that Inspired this one to be in the making

  • Unreal Engine: Unreal Engine is a powerful and versatile game development platform created by Epic Games, enabling developers to create high-quality 2D and 3D games, simulations, and visual experiences across various platforms.
  • The Day Before: is an open-world MMO survival game set in a post-apocalyptic America where players scavenge for resources, fight zombies and hostile survivors, and collaborate to survive in a dynamic environment.
  • H1Z1: is a multiplayer battle royale game that combines survival and crafting elements, allowing players to scavenge for weapons and resources while competing against each other in a shrinking play area until only one player or team remains.
  • 7 days to die: an open-world survival horror game that blends first-person shooter, survival, and tower defense mechanics, where players must gather resources, craft items, and build fortifications to survive against waves of zombies that become increasingly challenging every seven in-game days.

Survive the Undead, Outsmart the Living

Whispering Dead transports players into a post-apocalyptic world where every decision and every step matters. In this unforgiving landscape, zombies aren’t the only danger—wildlife, rival survivors, and even the changing seasons present unique challenges. Rather than relying on brute force, survival in Whispering Dead demands resourcefulness and strategic thinking. Players must scavenge for supplies, craft essential tools, and use the environment to their advantage, all while avoiding detection from the relentless undead. Stealth, patience, and caution are your most valuable weapons.

Adapt, Evolve, and Endure in a Changing World

As time passes, the world evolves around you. Power outages will plunge cities into darkness, and the seasons will affect everything from zombie behavior to available resources. Spring may bring a bounty of food, but Winter’s harsh cold could mean death if you’re unprepared. With a massive open world to explore—featuring cities, forests, and rural outposts—you’ll have to constantly adapt to stay ahead of the dangers lurking in every corner. Welcome to Whispering Dead, where the only certainty is that death is never far away.
